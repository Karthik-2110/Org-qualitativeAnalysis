Organic qualitative analysis

In this epidemic era, the field of computer-based learning tools is booming. In the discipline of chemistry, there are many unknown regions. The development of a new online application for a virtual organic lab for students studying Msc at Indian universities is a pressing requirement. The use of a web tool like this makes organic qualitative analysis easier to comprehend. The programme should be simple to use and can be referred to at any moment for a thorough knowledge. The application creates the impression of being in a genuine laboratory, complete with animations and visuals of chemical reactions.
